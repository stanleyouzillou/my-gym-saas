# Security, Privacy & Compliance

- **Threat modeling** per bounded context.
- **AuthN/Z**: least privilege, role/attr‑based; enforce at boundaries; avoid domain leakage of tokens.
- **Data protection**: encryption at rest/in transit, PII minimization, retention windows.
- **Supply chain**: pin deps, vulnerability scanning, SBOM.
- **Compliance**: audit trails via domain events; privacy modes & data deletion workflows.
